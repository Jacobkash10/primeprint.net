U.S. Passport Photo Specifications

📏 Size2 x 2 inches (51 x 51 mm)
🧑 Head SizeBetween 1 – 1.4 inches (25–35 mm) chin to top of head
🎨 BackgroundSolid white or off-white
📷 QualityHigh resolution, not blurry or pixelated
👕 ClothingNo uniforms, avoid white tops
👓 GlassesNot allowed (since Nov 2016)
😐 ExpressionNeutral face, both eyes open
💡 LightingNo shadows, face evenly lit
🛑 HeadgearOnly allowed for religious/medical reasons
